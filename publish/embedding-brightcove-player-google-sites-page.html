Embedding a Brightcove Player on a Google Sites Page

{{ page.description }}

Google Sites is a web page creation tool offered by Google. Brightcove Players can easily be embedded in Google Sites pages.

To embed a Brightcove Player on a Google Sites page, follow these steps:

  1. Locate the Google Sites page you want to add a Brightcove Player to.
  2. Click the Edit page button.
  3. A toolbar will appear at the top of the page. Click the <HTML> button.
  4. Paste in your Standard (iframe) player publishing code and click Update.
  5. The page will update and display a Google Gadget for the iframe.
  6. Click Save to save the changes. The video will be displayed in the page.
