DRM Output Protection Settings

{{ page.description }}

Configurable settings

The default settings for VOD DRM output are set by Brightcove and updated on a regular basis to reflect settings recommended by the vendors and most popular with DRM users. In most cases, the default settings should work for you.

If you have special needs and wish to alter the output settings for your account, the following settings can be modified:

  • License Type - persistent or non-persistent
  • License Expiration - whether video or account level
  • Default Security Level
  • Analog Output Protection
  • Digital Output Protection

If you wish to modify any of these settings for your account, contact your Account Manager.

Note: Brightcove currently does not support video-only or audio-only DRM playback.
