Video Cloud Basics: Uploading Video Poster & Thumbnail Images

In this topic you will learn how to upload poster and thumbnail images using the Media module.
 

Steps

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. Click on a video title to open the video properties page.
  3. Click Edit in the IMAGES section.
  4. Click Browse and select a new poster image.
  5. Click Browse and select a new thumbnail image.
  6. Click Save.

Note that it may take a minute for the processing to complete and for the images to appear in the Media module.
